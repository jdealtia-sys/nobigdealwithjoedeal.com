    Got Questions?

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Straight answers about roofing, siding, gutters, insurance, and working with Joe. No runaround — just the facts.

    Most homeowner's insurance policies cover storm-related damage including hail, wind, and falling debris. Joe will come out, inspect the damage, and help you determine if you have a valid claim before you file anything. We document everything — photos, measurements, damage reports — and work directly with your adjuster to make the process as smooth as possible.
    We handle the heavy lifting. Joe will walk you through every step — from the initial inspection and damage documentation to meeting with the adjuster and making sure the scope of work is approved correctly. We've helped dozens of homeowners maximize their claims and avoid underpayment. You won't be left figuring it out alone.
    Every job we do is backed by a 5-year labor warranty — meaning if anything goes wrong with our installation, we come back and fix it, no questions asked. On top of that, roofing shingles carry a 30-year manufacturer's warranty and siding installations come with a 50-year manufacturer's warranty through brands like Polaris and Royal.
    Yes — No Big Deal Home Solutions is fully licensed and insured. This protects you as a homeowner from any liability during the project. We can provide proof of insurance before any work begins. Never hire a contractor who can't show you this documentation.
    Most residential roof replacements are completed in a single day. Larger or more complex roofs may take two days. We work with multiple crews so we're not stretched thin across too many jobs at once. You'll get a clear timeline before we start, and we keep you updated throughout the project.
    Gutter replacements on a standard home are usually done in a single day. Siding jobs vary depending on the size of the home and the material — a full siding replacement typically takes 2–5 days. Repairs on both are often completed the same day we show up. We'll give you an honest timeline upfront so you can plan accordingly.
    For roofing we primarily install Owens Corning and Atlas shingles — two of the most trusted names in the industry, known for durability and long warranty coverage. For siding, we work with Polaris and Royal, and offer vinyl, shake, dutch lap, reverse board batten styles. We can also accommodate specific material requests — just ask.
    We install seamless K-style gutters in both 5-inch and 6-inch sizes. Seamless gutters are cut on-site to fit your home exactly, which means fewer joints, fewer leaks, and less maintenance than standard sectional gutters. The 6-inch option is great for larger roofs or areas with heavy rainfall. We also offer gutter guard installation to keep debris out.
    Inspections and estimates are always free. Just call, text, or fill out the form on this page — Joe will get back to you fast, usually same day. He'll come out, take a look, and give you a straight honest quote with no pressure and no upselling. If a repair is all you need, that's exactly what we'll tell you.
    Yes — we understand that a roof replacement or major exterior project is a significant investment. Financing options are available to help spread the cost. Ask Joe about current financing programs when you get your estimate. We want quality home protection to be accessible, not a financial burden.
    Never. Honesty is the foundation of this business. If a repair is the right call, we'll tell you that — even if a replacement would make us more money. Plenty of contractors skip repairs because there's less profit in them. We take on repairs that others won't, because we'd rather earn your trust for the long term than make a quick buck today.
    We're based in the Goshen / Cincinnati, Ohio area and serve communities throughout Southwest Ohio and Northern Kentucky — including Milford, Batavia, Loveland, Mason, Lebanon, Blue Ash, Indian Hill, Amelia, and more. Not sure if we cover your area? Give Joe a call — chances are we can make it work.
