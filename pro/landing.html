FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Is this built for insurance restoration or retail roofing? +
Both. NBD Pro supports full workflows for insurance restoration (claims management, adjuster coordination, damage documentation) and retail roofing (estimates, service plans, preventative maintenance). You can toggle between insurance and cash modes in the CRM pipeline, and the Academy covers processes for both.
Can I use this on my phone in the field? +
Yes. NBD Pro is a progressive web app (PWA) built for mobile-first workflows. It works offline, syncs when you have connection, and integrates directly with your phone's camera. You don't need an app store download — just visit the URL and add to your home screen.
How is this different from JobNimbus or AccuLynx? +
Built by a contractor with $10M+ in claims experience. Priced 1/3 less than competitors. Includes training (Real Deal Academy) built-in. No complex integrations required — D2D tracking, estimates, and CRM are all unified. Designed for the roofing workflow, not adapted from generic project management.
Can my whole team use it? +
Yes. Foundation plan supports 1 user. Blueprint and Professional allow team invites and full collaboration. You can assign Academy courses to team members and track their progress. All team members see the same pipeline, leads, and projects in real-time.
Is my data secure? +
Absolutely. We use Firebase for backend infrastructure with encryption in transit and at rest. All customer data is encrypted, regularly backed up, and compliant with SOC 2 standards. We never share or sell your data, and you can export everything anytime.
What if I need help getting started? +
We've got you covered. Every plan includes access to an interactive onboarding wizard that walks you through setup step-by-step. Blueprint and Professional plans get priority email/chat support. The Real Deal Academy has video walkthroughs for every feature. And you can reach out anytime — we're here to help you succeed.
