Complete Gutter Replacement Solutions

Gutters are one of the most overlooked yet critical components of your home's water management system. When gutters fail—whether from age, damage, or poor installation—water overflows and saturates the ground around your foundation, leading to basement leaks, foundation damage, and costly repairs. New, properly functioning gutters are essential for protecting your home's foundation and structural integrity.

Oaks Roofing & Construction specializes in seamless aluminum gutters that are custom-fabricated on-site to fit your roof's dimensions perfectly. Seamless gutters eliminate the weak points where traditional sectional gutters join together, reducing the likelihood of leaks and providing superior performance in heavy rain events.

Foundation Protection

Properly functioning gutters direct water away from your foundation, preventing costly water damage and structural issues.

Custom-Fit Installation

Premium Seamless Gutter Systems

5-Inch Aluminum Gutters

Standard 5-inch gutters handle most residential applications effectively. They're suitable for typical roofline lengths and perform well in moderate to heavy rainfall. Cost-effective and widely used, 5-inch gutters provide reliable water management for most Goshen homes.

6-Inch Aluminum Gutters

For homes with larger roof areas or in regions with frequent heavy rainfall, 6-inch gutters provide 40% greater water-carrying capacity. They prevent overflow in intense storms and are ideal for steep-pitch roofs that channel large volumes of water quickly.

Professional Downspout Routing

We design downspout layouts that efficiently direct water away from your home's foundation. This includes proper sizing, placement, and extensions to ensure water disperses at least 4-6 feet from your foundation.

Seamless Construction Advantages

Unlike sectional gutters with multiple joints where leaks develop, our seamless gutters are one continuous piece. This eliminates weak points and provides superior water-carrying performance and longevity.

Gutter Guard Options

Leaves, twigs, and debris accumulation is one of the primary reasons gutters fail. Gutter guards reduce maintenance needs and keep water flowing freely through your gutter system.

Mesh Gutter Guards

Fine-mesh screens allow water to pass while blocking debris. They're effective for most situations and relatively affordable. Occasionally need cleaning of accumulated surface debris.

Reverse-Curve Guards

Water flows over the curved surface into the gutter while leaves and debris slide off. This system is highly effective and requires less frequent maintenance than mesh guards.

No Guards Option

Some homeowners prefer traditional gutters without guards and perform regular maintenance. This is cost-effective upfront but requires annual cleaning.

Proper Water Management

New gutters are only part of the equation. Proper water management requires thoughtful system design and professional installation.

Sizing & Capacity

We calculate the right gutter size based on your roof area, pitch, and local rainfall data. This ensures your gutters handle heavy rains without overflow.

Slope & Drainage

Gutters must slope toward downspouts to ensure water flows properly. We install gutters with proper pitch to prevent standing water and debris accumulation.

Downspout Extensions

Downspouts extend beyond the foundation to disperse water away from your home. We typically use 4-6 foot extensions or connect to drainage systems as needed.
