Complete Roof Replacement Solutions

Your roof is one of the most important investments you can make in your home. Whether your current roof is aging, damaged by severe weather, or simply no longer providing adequate protection, Oaks Roofing & Construction specializes in complete roof replacement services that combine quality materials, expert craftsmanship, and industry-leading warranties.

A full roof replacement involves the complete removal of your existing roofing system down to the deck, followed by installation of a new, high-performance roof with modern underlayment, flashing, and finishing details. This comprehensive approach ensures maximum protection against leaks, water damage, and weather-related deterioration.

Comprehensive Protection

Full tear-off installation provides superior water protection and extends roof life by 5-10 years compared to overlay installations.

Energy Efficiency

New roofing materials reflect more sunlight, reducing cooling costs and improving your home's overall energy performance.

Premium Materials for Lasting Performance

Architectural Shingles

We exclusively install premium architectural (dimensional) shingles that offer superior durability, aesthetic appeal, and longevity compared to standard three-tab shingles. These shingles are engineered to resist wind, impact, and UV degradation, with many carrying 50-year manufacturer warranties.

Advanced Underlayment Systems

Beneath every new roof, we install synthetic underlayment that provides an additional moisture barrier while allowing vapor transmission. This creates a secondary defense against leaks and ice dams while extending the life of your roof deck.

Professional Grade Flashing

All roof penetrations—including chimneys, vents, skylights, and valleys—are sealed with professional-grade flashing materials installed to prevent leaks at these critical points.

Proper Ventilation

Ridge vents, soffit vents, and gable vents are installed or upgraded to ensure proper attic ventilation, which extends roof life and prevents heat and moisture buildup that can cause premature shingle deterioration.

Our Roof Replacement Process

Every roof replacement project follows our proven, professional process to ensure quality installation and customer satisfaction. We start with a comprehensive inspection and detailed estimate, then schedule your installation at your convenience.

Complete Tear-Off

We safely remove all existing roofing materials down to the roof deck, allowing us to inspect the structural integrity and address any underlying issues before installing your new roof.

Deck Preparation

Any damaged or compromised deck boards are replaced, and the entire deck is inspected to ensure a solid foundation for your new roof system.

New Roof Installation

Professional installation of underlayment, flashing, ice & water shield, starter strips, and architectural shingles according to manufacturer specifications.
