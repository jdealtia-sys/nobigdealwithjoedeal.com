Common Roof Repairs We Handle

Leak Detection & Repair

Water leaks are the most common roof issue. We use advanced detection methods to locate the exact source of leaks, which is often several feet away from where water appears inside your home. Once identified, we seal the leak with permanent repairs that keep water out.

Missing & Damaged Shingles

Wind, weather, and age can leave your roof with missing or damaged shingles that expose the underlying layers to water and UV damage. We replace damaged shingles with color-matched alternatives and re-seal the area to prevent water intrusion.

Storm Damage Repairs

High winds, hail, and fallen branches can cause significant roof damage. We assess storm damage comprehensively and coordinate with insurance companies to ensure proper claim documentation and coverage.

Flashing & Penetration Repairs

Flashing around chimneys, vents, skylights, and other penetrations often fails before the rest of your roof. We replace deteriorated flashing with professional-grade materials to seal these vulnerable points.
