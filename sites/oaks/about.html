Who We Are

A local roofing and exterior company built on honesty, quality, and fair pricing.

Our Story

Oaks Roofing & Construction started with a simple insight: we could build something better. Our founders spent years in roofing sales, learning the industry from every angle—the good practices, the shortcuts, and the way things should be done.

We decided to start our own company focused on one principle: deliver quality work at prices that make sense. By keeping overhead low and staying hands-on, we pass savings directly to our customers without compromising on craftsmanship.

What began as a single-trade roofing operation has grown into a full-service home exterior contractor. Today, we offer roof replacement and repair, siding installation and repair, gutter services, and storm damage restoration—all backed by our 5-year warranty.
