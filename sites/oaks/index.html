Who We Are

Oaks Roofing & Construction started with a simple idea: deliver quality exterior services at prices that make sense. We began in roofing sales, learned the industry inside and out, and decided to build our own company dedicated to honest work and fair pricing.

Based in Goshen, Ohio, we've grown from a single-trade roofing company to a full-service exterior contractor. Our low overhead keeps costs down, and our hands-on approach ensures every job is done right. We're licensed, insured, and backed by a 5-year warranty.

When you choose Oaks, you're choosing a local team that stands behind its work and treats your home like our own.
