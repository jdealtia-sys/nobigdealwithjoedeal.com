Common questions

Hail Damage Insurance Claims FAQ — Cincinnati

How can I tell if my roof has hail damage? ▸
From the ground: dented gutters, chipped paint on trim, and dings on AC units or mailboxes. On the roof: random pattern of dark spots where granules are knocked off, exposing the asphalt mat underneath. Joe's inspection covers all of it.
Will hail damage affect my roof right away? ▸
Not always visually, but yes structurally. Hail cracks the fiberglass mat inside shingles, which accelerates aging. A roof with unrepaired hail damage will fail years before its expected lifespan.
What size hail causes damage? ▸
Hail as small as 1 inch (quarter-sized) can damage standard architectural shingles. The density and wind speed matter as much as size — wind-driven hail hits harder.
My neighbor got a new roof from their claim. Should I file too? ▸
If you're in the same area, probably yes. Hail storms affect entire neighborhoods. Joe offers free inspections — if there's no damage, he'll tell you honestly and you won't file an unnecessary claim.
