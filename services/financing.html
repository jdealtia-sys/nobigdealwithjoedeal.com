FAQ

Financing Questions.
Real Answers.

Will applying affect my credit score?

Improvifi uses a soft credit pull for the initial application which does not affect your credit score. A hard inquiry only occurs if you accept a financing offer and proceed.

What credit score do I need?

Improvifi works with a range of credit profiles. Joe will help you understand your options based on what's available. If financing isn't the right fit, he'll tell you honestly instead of pushing you into something that doesn't make sense.

Can I finance insurance work too?

Yes — financing can cover your deductible or any out-of-pocket portion of an insurance-covered project. This is one of the most common uses.

Is there a minimum project size for financing?

Generally financing makes the most sense for projects $3,000 and above. Ask Joe about your specific project and he'll give you a straight answer on whether it makes financial sense to finance.
