Common questions

Storm Damage Repair FAQ — Mason

How do I know if I have storm damage? ▸
You often can't tell from the ground. Hail damage shows as bruised or cracked shingles, missing granules, and dented gutters. Wind damage shows as lifted, creased, or missing shingles. Joe's free inspection catches what you can't see.
Should I call my insurance company first or get an inspection first? ▸
Get the inspection first. Joe documents everything with photos before you file, so your claim is backed by evidence from day one. Filing without documentation can result in a lower payout.
How long do I have to file a claim? ▸
In Ohio, most policies allow 1-2 years from the date of the storm, but sooner is always better. Evidence deteriorates, and insurance companies can argue that later damage was caused by wear, not the storm.
What if my claim gets denied? ▸
Joe has experience with denied claims and can help you file a supplement or request a re-inspection. Many denials are reversed when proper documentation is provided.
