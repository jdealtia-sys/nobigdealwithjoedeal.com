Fire, Water & Smoke Damage

When It's More Than
a Roofing Job.

Some jobs go beyond a roof replacement. Fire damage, water intrusion, and smoke damage affect the entire structure — and navigating the insurance claim for full restoration is a completely different process than a standard storm claim.

Joe Deal handles the exterior scope — roof, siding, gutters, fascia, soffit, decking — and works directly with United Restore for the full structural and interior restoration side. That means you're not managing two separate contractors and two separate insurance conversations. Joe coordinates it.

If you've had a fire, a severe water intrusion, or smoke damage, the first call you make matters. Joe documents the exterior damage scope immediately — before anything is disturbed — which protects your claim and ensures nothing gets missed by the adjuster.

  • Immediate exterior damage assessment and documentation
  • Full photo and written scope for insurance purposes
  • Roof, siding, soffit, fascia, and structural decking review
  • Direct coordination with United Restore for interior/structural work
  • Single point of contact through the entire claim
  • Supplement support if initial claim scope is incomplete

Why Documentation Timing Matters

Insurance adjusters are trained to minimize scope. The first 24-48 hours after a fire or major water event are critical — this is when damage is fully visible and before any temporary repairs disturb the evidence.

Joe shows up fast, documents everything, and makes sure your claim reflects the full reality of what happened — not just what the adjuster wants to write.
