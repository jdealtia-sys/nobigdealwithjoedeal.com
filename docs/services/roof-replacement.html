Questions & Answers

Roof Replacement FAQ

How long does a roof replacement take?
Most residential roof replacements in Cincinnati are completed in one day. Larger homes or complex rooflines may take two days. We don't leave a job unfinished — your roof will be weathertight every night regardless.
Does my homeowner's insurance cover a roof replacement?
If your roof was damaged by storm, hail, or wind, there's a good chance your insurance covers it. Ohio homeowners are often surprised how much their policy covers once it's properly documented. Joe will inspect your roof, document the damage, and tell you honestly whether you have a claim worth filing — before you do anything else.
What shingles do you use?
We install GAF Timberline shingles — carrying a lifetime limited manufacturer warranty on the materials. We carry architectural (dimensional) shingles as our standard and can also install impact-resistant options that may qualify for insurance discounts in Ohio.
Do you tear off the old roof or go over it?
We always do a full tear-off. Layering new shingles over old ones is faster and cheaper for the contractor, but it hides problems in the decking, adds unnecessary weight, and voids most manufacturer warranties. We don't cut corners like that.
How much does a roof replacement cost in Cincinnati?
Every roof is different — size, pitch, material, and condition of the decking all affect the price. What we can promise is that our quotes are fair and we'll explain every line item. Call Joe and get a real number, not a range designed to anchor your expectations high.
Do you serve areas outside of Cincinnati?
Yes — we serve Greater Cincinnati including Milford, Loveland, Mason, Batavia, Lebanon, Maineville, Blue Ash, Indian Hill, Amelia, and surrounding communities. If you're not sure if we cover your area, just call or text Joe.
