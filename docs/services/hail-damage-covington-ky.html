Hail Damage FAQ — Covington, KY

Does Kentucky homeowners insurance cover hail damage in Covington? ▸
Yes. Hail is a standard covered peril under Kentucky homeowners insurance policies — the same as Ohio. If hail caused the damage, your policy covers it subject to your deductible. Joe handles the full claim process from inspection through adjuster coordination and supplement negotiation.
How do I know if my Covington roof has hail damage? ▸
From the ground, look for dented gutters, chipped paint on fascia, and dings on AC units or any soft metal. On the roof, hail leaves a random pattern of dark spots — granules knocked off the shingle surface, exposing the asphalt mat underneath. Joe's free inspection covers the full assessment.
What hail size triggers a full roof replacement claim in Covington? ▸
Generally, 1-inch diameter hail (quarter-sized) or larger can cause functional damage to standard architectural shingles sufficient for a full replacement claim. The impact density across the roof and the age of the shingles are also factors. Joe documents impact density measurements that align with what Kentucky insurance adjusters use to determine scope.
My Covington neighborhood got hit by hail — should I get an inspection even if I don't see obvious damage? ▸
Yes. Hail damage is often not visible from the ground and may not cause leaks right away — but it compromises the shingle structure and accelerates aging. If your area was hit, Joe's free inspection will confirm whether damage occurred and whether a claim makes sense.
