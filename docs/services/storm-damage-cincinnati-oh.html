Storm Damage FAQ — Cincinnati

How soon after a storm should I get my Cincinnati roof inspected? ▸
Within 30 days — most Ohio insurance policies have claim filing windows. Don't wait until you see water inside your home.
What storm damage is typically covered by Cincinnati homeowner insurance? ▸
Wind, hail, and falling debris are standard covered perils. Joe documents the damage in terms that adjusters recognize and approve.
My Cincinnati neighbors are getting new roofs. Did we get hit by the same storm? ▸
Probably yes. Hail and wind storms affect entire neighborhoods. Joe can cross-reference storm dates for your area.
Will a storm damage claim raise my rates in Ohio? ▸
Filing a weather-related claim in Ohio typically does not result in non-renewal. Joe explains the trade-offs before you file anything.
