Who We Are

About Oaks Roofing & Construction

Our journey started in roofing sales, and over the years, we built a business focused on roofing and siding solutions that homeowners can count on. What began as a single trade evolved into a full-service roofing company with the ability to offer both repair and replacement at competitive prices.

Unlike larger companies with heavy overhead, we're able to pass savings on to our customers and provide fair, transparent pricing. Based in Goshen, OH, we take pride in serving local homeowners with a level of care and attention others often overlook.

We believe in fixing what can be fixed. That mindset, paired with our trade skills and hands-on service, gives our customers reliable results every time. Our team handles everything from full roof replacements and storm damage repairs to siding installation and gutter replacement.
