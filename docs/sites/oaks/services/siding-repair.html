Expert Siding Repair Services

Your home's siding faces constant exposure to weather, temperature changes, UV rays, and physical impacts. Over time, this can result in cracks, holes, warping, and separation that compromise your home's weather protection and appearance. When siding damage occurs, professional repair is essential to prevent water infiltration and further deterioration.

Oaks Roofing & Construction provides comprehensive siding repair services for all material types—vinyl, fiber cement, wood, and composite. Whether you're dealing with cosmetic damage, structural concerns, or moisture-related issues, our experienced technicians diagnose the problem and provide lasting repairs that restore your home's protective envelope.

Common Siding Issues We Repair

Panel & Board Damage

Cracked, broken, or dented siding panels allow moisture to reach the substrate underneath. We replace damaged panels with color-matched materials, restoring both appearance and protection.

Weather & Impact Damage

Hail, wind, fallen branches, and accidents can damage siding. We assess impact damage comprehensively and coordinate with insurance if applicable.

Moisture & Rot Issues

Wood siding and damaged substrate can develop rot and moisture problems. We address the underlying cause and implement proper repairs with moisture barriers.

Cracked & Split Siding

Cracks in fiber cement or wood siding allow water intrusion. We repair cracks with specialized techniques that prevent water from reaching the substrate.

Types of Siding Damage We Handle

Vinyl Siding Issues

Vinyl can become brittle in cold weather and crack from impact. UV exposure can also cause fading and color degradation. We repair damaged vinyl with color-matched replacement panels that maintain your home's appearance.

Fiber Cement Problems

Although highly durable, fiber cement can develop cracks, especially around penetrations. We seal cracks with specialized compounds that maintain the material's protective properties.

Wood Siding Damage

Wood siding is susceptible to rot, splitting, and pest damage. We repair damaged boards, treat moisture issues, and apply finishes that protect against future deterioration.
