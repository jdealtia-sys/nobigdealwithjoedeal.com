Siding Materials for Every Style & Budget

Premium Vinyl Siding

Our vinyl siding options offer superior durability, fade resistance, and a wide variety of colors and styles. With foam-backed panels for improved insulation, vinyl provides excellent weather protection and requires minimal maintenance. It's impact-resistant and won't rot or warp like wood.

Fiber Cement Siding

For homeowners seeking authentic wood appearance with superior durability, fiber cement is the ideal choice. This engineered material resists rot, insects, and fire while providing a sophisticated, natural wood aesthetic that can be painted any color. It's backed by 25-year warranties.

Board & Batten Styles

Create a classic or contemporary look with board & batten siding profiles. This distinctive style features vertical boards with battens between, offering architectural interest and visual depth that makes a statement.

Custom Trim & Accents

We coordinate siding with custom trim work, fascia, and soffit to create a cohesive, professionally finished appearance that elevates your entire home's aesthetic.

Benefits of New Siding

Enhanced Curb Appeal

New siding instantly transforms your home's appearance. A fresh exterior makes a powerful first impression and can increase your home's perceived value by 5-10%.

Better Weather Protection

Modern siding materials are engineered to resist moisture infiltration, UV damage, and extreme weather. Your home's structure and interior are better protected from the elements.

Reduced Maintenance

Unlike wood siding, modern vinyl and fiber cement require minimal maintenance. No painting, staining, or extensive upkeep—just occasional cleaning.

Energy Savings

Foam-backed siding improves insulation value, reducing heat loss in winter and heat gain in summer for lower energy bills year-round.

Our Installation Process

Comprehensive Assessment

We inspect your home's exterior to assess the condition of the underlying structure and identify any damage that needs repair before new siding installation.

Material Selection & Design

Together, we'll select the right siding material and color that complements your home's architecture and your personal style. We'll show you samples and discuss durability and maintenance.

Professional Installation

Our certified installers remove old siding, repair any damaged substrate, install house wrap for moisture protection, and carefully install your new siding according to manufacturer specifications.

Trim & Finishing

We complete the job with professional trim installation around windows, doors, and corners, plus soffit and fascia work for a polished, finished appearance.
