The Fine Print

Rules, honestly stated.

  • Open to homeowners in NBD's Greater Cincinnati service area (Hamilton, Clermont, Warren, Butler, Campbell, Kenton, Boone counties).
  • One entry per household. Duplicate submissions are consolidated, not disqualified.
  • Nominee must be the legal homeowner of the property at the time of installation.
  • Finalists' roof condition is confirmed by a free NBD inspection. The inspection happens regardless of whether you're selected.
  • Winner is selected at NBD's sole discretion. This is a gift, not a contest — I'm looking for real need, not the best essay.
  • Full replacement covers materials, labor, tear-off, underlayment, flashing, and a standard NBD warranty. Structural damage beyond a normal replacement is discussed ahead of work.
  • If the selected home is structurally unsound or insured for a covered replacement already, NBD may select a different finalist.
  • NBD reserves the right to document the gift on social media. The homeowner's privacy preferences come first — faces and exact addresses only shared with explicit consent.
