Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. Here's exactly how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Last Updated: April 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to nobigdealwithjoedeal.com (the "Website"), operated by No Big Deal Home Solutions, a home services contractor serving Greater Cincinnati, Ohio. By using this Website and providing your information, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

When you submit a free estimate request, contact form, or communicate with us, we collect:

  • Name, phone number, email address
  • Physical address or zip code
  • Description of your project or service needed
  • Any other information you voluntarily provide

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our Website, we automatically collect:

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • IP address
  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring webpage and links clicked
  • Device identifiers and similar technical data

Information from Third Parties

We may receive information from our service providers (see section 5 below) to help us understand how you use our Website and to improve our services.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Responding to your estimate requests and inquiries
  • Providing quotes and scheduling consultations
  • Processing payments and managing customer accounts
  • Sending follow-up communications about your project
  • Improving our Website and services
  • Marketing and promotional communications (with your consent)
  • Complying with legal obligations
  • Preventing fraud and ensuring security

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to understand how you use our Website and to improve your experience. Cookies are small files stored on your device that help us remember your preferences and analyze usage patterns.

Types of Cookies

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the Website to function properly
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use the Website
  • Marketing Cookies: Track your interactions to deliver relevant content

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect Website functionality. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or alert you when cookies are being sent.

4. Third-Party Service Providers

We use third-party services to operate and improve our Website. These partners may collect and use your information as described below:

Firebase

We use Google Firebase for data storage, authentication, and database management. Firebase securely stores customer inquiry data and project information. Firebase is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics (Measurement ID: G-8PG7N9Q3DL) to track Website traffic and user behavior. This helps us understand what's working and improve your experience. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect data and is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

Stripe

When you make a payment, we use Stripe to process credit card and payment information. Stripe handles all payment processing and does not store your full payment details on our servers. Stripe is PCI compliant and subject to Stripe's Privacy Policy.

Email & Communication Services

We use Resend to deliver transactional email (estimate confirmations, appointment reminders, receipts) and Twilio to send SMS messages when you have opted in. These providers process your email address, phone number, and message content on our behalf.

AI-Assisted Features

Some features (property intelligence, proposal drafting, objection handling assistance) use the Anthropic API (Claude). When you interact with AI features we may send the addresses, notes, or questions you submit to Anthropic for processing. Anthropic does not use this data to train their models under our API agreement.

E-Signature

Contracts we send for signature are processed through BoldSign (Syncfusion). BoldSign receives your name, email address, and the contract document so that you can sign electronically. The signed document and the signature event log are stored in our records and with BoldSign.

Roof Measurements

Aerial roof measurement reports are sourced from the providers below. To produce a report we share the property address with the chosen provider; in return we receive roof dimensions and imagery. No personal information beyond the property address is transmitted.

  • HOVER (primary)
  • EagleView (fallback)
  • Nearmap (fallback)

Property Intelligence

Public county records, ownership data, and parcel shapes are looked up through Regrid. Only the property address is shared to perform these lookups.

Weather & Storm Data

Historical hail reports and storm prediction data are sourced from the free National Weather Service / NOAA feeds and, optionally, from HailTrace (paid subscription tier). Only approximate property coordinates are shared.

Appointment Scheduling

When you book an inspection through our homeowner portal, Cal.com processes your name, email address, phone number, and selected time slot on our behalf.

Security & Monitoring

We use Sentry for error monitoring and Cloudflare Turnstile to distinguish humans from bots on public forms. Sentry receives anonymized error events (PII such as email and phone is stripped before submission). Turnstile uses a privacy-preserving challenge and does not track you across sites.

We do not sell, share, or rent your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes. Our third-party partners may use your information only to perform services on our behalf and are bound by confidentiality agreements.

5. Text Messages, Calls, and TCPA Compliance

If you provide your phone number, you may receive text messages or calls from No Big Deal Home Solutions about your estimate request, project updates, or follow-up communications.

TCPA Notice: By providing your phone number and submitting an estimate request, you consent to receive SMS text messages and phone calls from No Big Deal Home Solutions at the number you provided, regarding your home service estimate and project. Standard message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to any text message to opt out of SMS communications. You can also call us at (859) 420-7382 to update your preferences.

We comply with the Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA) and will not send marketing texts or calls without your explicit consent. You may withdraw consent at any time.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services, fulfill legal obligations, and maintain business records.

  • Contact inquiries: Retained for 3 years after the estimate request
  • Payment records: Retained for 7 years for tax and accounting purposes
  • Marketing communications: Retained until you unsubscribe
  • Analytics data: Typically retained for 12 months

If you wish to delete your information, contact us at jd@nobigdealwithjoedeal.com or (859) 420-7382. We will delete or anonymize your data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal or business purposes.

7. Data Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/SSL)
  • Secure data storage in Firebase
  • Restricted access to customer data
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Secure payment processing through Stripe (PCI compliant)

While we use reasonable security measures, no online system is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information. Please use strong passwords and do not share your login credentials.

8. Your Privacy Rights and Choices

Access and Correction

You have the right to access, review, and update your personal information. Contact us at jd@nobigdealwithjoedeal.com to request your data.

Opt-Out of Communications

You can opt out of marketing emails by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email. You can opt out of text messages by replying STOP. We will still send you transactional messages about your estimate or project unless you request to stop all communications.

Do Not Track

Some browsers include a Do Not Track feature. Our Website does not currently respond to Do Not Track signals, but you can limit tracking through your browser settings and cookie controls.

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information is collected, to delete your information, to opt out of the "sale" of your information, and to non-discrimination for exercising your rights. Contact us to exercise these rights.

Other State Privacy Laws

Similar rights may apply under privacy laws in Virginia, Colorado, Connecticut, Utah, and other states. We will honor requests under applicable state privacy laws.

9. Children's Privacy

Our Website is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child has provided personal information, we will delete that information immediately. Parents who believe their child has submitted information should contact us at jd@nobigdealwithjoedeal.com.

10. External Links

Our Website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external websites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before submitting any information.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the Website following changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
