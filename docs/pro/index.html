FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Is this built for insurance restoration or retail roofing? +
Both. NBD Pro supports full workflows for insurance restoration (claims management, adjuster coordination, damage documentation) and retail roofing (estimates, service plans, preventative maintenance). You can toggle between insurance and cash modes in the CRM pipeline, and the Academy covers processes for both.
Can I use this on my phone in the field? +
Yes. NBD Pro is a progressive web app (PWA) built for mobile-first workflows. It runs right in your browser, works great one-handed on a phone, and uses your phone's camera directly for photos. No app store download — just visit the URL and add it to your home screen.
How is this different from JobNimbus or AccuLynx? +
It's built by a working roofer, not a software company that decided roofing looked profitable. It's a fraction of the price of the big platforms. Training (the Real Deal Academy) is built in. And D2D tracking, estimates, and CRM are unified in one tool — not bolted onto generic project-management software.
Can my whole team use it? +
Yes. Free and Solo are single-user. Crew includes up to 5 team seats (additional seats are $39/mo each), and Scale is unlimited. You can assign Academy courses to team members and track their progress, and everyone sees the same pipeline, leads, and projects.
Is my data secure? +
I take it seriously. NBD Pro is built on Google Firebase — SOC 2-compliant infrastructure with encryption in transit and at rest. I never share or sell your data, and you can request a copy of your data anytime. Your data stays yours, even if you cancel.
What if I need help getting started? +
I've got you covered. Every plan includes the interactive onboarding wizard that walks you through setup step-by-step, plus the Real Deal Academy walkthroughs. Crew and Scale get priority support, and you can email jd@nobigdealwithjoedeal.com anytime — I'm here to help you succeed.
