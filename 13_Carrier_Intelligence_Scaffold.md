### State Farm

**Default Posture**
- Repair-first basis. Standard practice.
- Timeline: typically 2-3 weeks to first determination.
- Threshold language: 30-50% field damage triggers replacement consideration (per Chris's transcript and Jo's field experience).
- DOI complaint receptivity: Low. They have too many complaints to care about new ones.
- Internal escalation: Medium effectiveness. Field claim reps may push back on adjusters who hold things up.

**How they treat NTS / third-party reports**
- Default: typically "we don't accept those" or silence.
- What gets review: clarifying it's evidence not an ask, citing burden-flip language ("are you suggesting you refuse to review evidence on an active claim?").

## Fields to populate over time

When you close a claim and update the Win/Loss Tracker, also update this sheet:

1. **Adjuster encountered** — name, contact, pattern notes
2. **Common moves they pulled** — reference the moves index
3. **What worked / didn't work** — short notes
4. **Timeline** — days to resolution
5. **Threshold language** — anything specific they said about repair-vs-replace thresholds

Quarterly: look across the sheet, identify cross-carrier patterns ("State Farm and Allstate both lean on right-contractor trap; USAA leans on stonewalling"). Use those patterns to refine Wave 1's email templates and the doctrine doc.

## How to wire this into NBD Pro

**Firestore schema:**

```
/carriers/{carrier_id}
{
  name: string,
  default_posture: object {
    repair_first: boolean,
    timeline_days_avg: number,
    threshold_language: string,
    doi_receptivity: 'high' | 'medium' | 'low' | 'none',
    escalation_effectiveness: 'high' | 'medium' | 'low' | 'none'
  },
  patterns: {
    nts_default_response: string,
    itel_dmi_quirks: string,
    common_moves: array<string> // ref to moves index IDs
  },
  what_works: array<string>,
  what_doesnt_work: array<string>,
  adjusters: array<{
    name: string,
    phone: string,
    email: string,
    pattern_notes: string,
    claims_won: number,
    claims_lost: number
  }>,
  claim_notes: array<{
    claim_id: string,
    date: timestamp,
    outcome: string,
    key_learning: string
  }>,
  escalation: {
    main_phone: string,
    what_works: string
  },
  last_updated: timestamp
}
```

**UI:** Add a "/carriers" route in NBD Pro. List view with all carriers. Click into one for detail. Edit-in-place fields. Pull this up before any new claim.

**Auto-population:** When you close a claim in the Win/Loss Tracker, prompt: "Update [carrier name] intelligence with what you learned?" — pre-fills relevant fields.
