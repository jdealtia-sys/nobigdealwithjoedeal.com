# No Big Deal Home Solutions — Review Collection Templates

Use these text templates to request Google reviews from customers. Copy/paste and personalize with the customer's name and the service performed.

---

## Right After Job Completion

> Hey [NAME]! Joe from No Big Deal Home Solutions. Just wanted to say thanks for trusting us with your [roof/siding/gutters]. If you've got 30 seconds, a quick Google review would mean the world: nobigdealwithjoedeal.com/review

---

## One Week Follow-Up

> Hi [NAME], Joe Deal here. Quick check — everything looking good with the [roof/siding] we did last week? If so, I'd really appreciate a Google review when you get a chance: nobigdealwithjoedeal.com/review

---

## After Insurance Claim

> Hey [NAME]! Glad we could get your [roof/siding] taken care of through insurance. If the experience was smooth, a Google review helps other homeowners find us: nobigdealwithjoedeal.com/review

---

## Tips for Maximum Reviews

- Send the text within 1–2 hours of completing the job (while the customer is still impressed)
- Follow up once at the one-week mark if they haven't reviewed yet
- Never send more than two follow-ups total
- Always personalize — use their name and the specific work you did
- The review link at nobigdealwithjoedeal.com/review redirects to Google
